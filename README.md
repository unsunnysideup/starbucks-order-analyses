# Starbucks Order Analysis: Your purchase tells everything

This is a multi-step project analyzing customer and sales through a comprehensive exploration of 100,000 Starbucks orders across the span of 2 years. The following questions will be addressed:

1. What do customers' orders tell us about themselves and what implications do they have for Starbucks?
2. What factors contribute to churn? 
3. What do current time series data tell us about the the growth of sales and orders in the near future?
4. How do algorithms and systems used in addressing the prior three questions differ and compare?
 
## Dataset
The [dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/likithagedipudi/starbucks-customer-ordering-patterns/code]) is sourced from Kaggle and was created by Likitha Gedipudi. In brief, it contains 20 variables spanning from time and location to customer's order expense and waiting time. It models order patterns seen in Starbucks franchises.
